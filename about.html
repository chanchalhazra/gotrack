Welcome to SinnoFlex Inc.

SinnoFlex is a leading technology consulting firm serving clients globally. We are experts in designing, building and delivering business-driven technology solutions. We help our clients gain competitive advantage by using Internet-based technologies to make their businesses more responsive to market opportunities and threats, strengthen relationships with customers, suppliers and partners, improve productivity and reduce information technology costs.

At SinnoFlex, we place strong emphasis on building lasting relationships with clients and being 100% referenceable. Our focus is earn 80% of our revenue was from repeat business. We believe this reflects our commitment to quality consulting and the long-term partnerships we build with our clients.

Our Team

Our Leadership team consists of veteran industry executives with decades of program/project management, implementation and product development experience. The team brings strong industry knowledge, business transformation experience and business process expertise. Our leaders have strong passion for emerging technology and business models. A critical element is our understanding of Enterprise Resource Planning, Customer Relationship Management and Business Intelligence best practices. The team has extensive expertise in High Tech, Retail, Manufacturing and Financial industries. We always strive for simplicity and speed of execution. We have a team of advisors who contribute heavily to our strategy and growth.

Our consultants in technology services practice are well versed with latest technology stack to develop mobile(Andriod, iOS, native, hybrid, HTML5), web applications, Custom enterprise applications both cloud based as well as on premise with right hookups for social media connect (Facebook,Orkut,Twitter, Google+, LinkedIn) for enterprises. Additionally our consultants have the important skill to develop deeper integrations with multiple enterprise applications to enabling business functions/processes on mobile device. These consultants are able to leverage existing APIs or create multi-use APIs if required to integrate with enterprise systems like CRM, ERP, BI, product catalog, pricing and promotions-engine. Our consultants in Business Solutions practice have many years of experience to implement CRM, ERP and BI applications globally. Our team members have expertise on implementing Sales and Service cloud in Salesforce, Oracle ERP application for finance and manufacturing functions, Netsuite for finance, OBIEE for business analytics
